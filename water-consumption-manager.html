“In Mexico the scarcity of water is worrisome, according to NASA the 80% of the country is in drought and has 33% of its water reserves.”

From 2001 to 2015 the consumption of water from public supply according to SEMARNAT data has grown by 30.7%.

“In Jalisco, according to SIAPA data, water consumption per inhabitant is in 221 liters, while the WHO recommends 50 to 100 liters.”

Solution

It is intended to solve the problem through a system responsible for measuring home water consumption, sending this information to a cloud database to interpret the data within an application and be displayed to the user.

Prototype

The fundamental part of our prototype will be the goals system to encourage the user to reduce their consumption, creating awareness in users when exceeding the limits of use recommended.

The prototype consisting of an electronic stage and a web application.
The sensor is installed where you want to measure the consumption and the application presents an intuitive and clean user interface.

Electronics

This part is composed of 3 elements:

  • A high-precision flow sensor to measure up to 30 liters/min.
  • Microcontroller that receives sensor data and connects to the cloud database.
  • Visual indicator to show alerts and the current status of the system.

Application

The application will allow to review the consumption in real time, the limit consumption, the history of the week, start or stop the consumption, badges achieved, among other sections.

3D Enclosure

Design inspired by current meters that allows easy installation for the user. It has a view of the screen and holes in the bottom that prevent damage to the electronics due to leaks.
